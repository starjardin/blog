Life in Mahanoro

Environment

In general, Mahanoro is a nice town because it is such a green space, the environment is wonderful. Mahanoro has lots of fruits such as jack fruit . Needless to say, There are lots of places which are good for picnic one of my favourite is Ambatolatabatra .

People

Peopele here are welcoming. The serenity is good, people love to interact to each other and they love sharing news everyday. If you are a guest you will never be lost because people are helping each other.
